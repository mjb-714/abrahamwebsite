The family behind the name

Abraham’s Funeral Home was founded by Willie Abraham in 1924. His legacy of compassionate care was carried on by his grandson Barrie, and then by his great-granddaughters Bronwyn and Megan, who now help lead the business. Mark, Megan’s husband, continues the tradition as Funeral Home Manager, ensuring the Abraham family values remain at the heart of everything we do.
